About Us

Robotics Education Across India

Seiva Robotics & Technologies is an emerging technology startup focused on Internet of Things (IoT), Robotics, Automation, and Research & Development. We are committed to bridging the gap between academic knowledge and real-world industrial applications through practical and innovation-driven learning.

Our startup is built with a vision to empower students, institutions, and industries by providing hands-on technology training and smart automation solutions. We travel across schools and colleges, bringing cutting-edge robotics education directly to students.

Basic Robotics for Schools
